    Day1

    Unveiling content for you

    On the first day itself you’ll know the growth potential of content marketing. You’ll find out what great content marketers, all over the world, are doing. The different kinds of content successfully, millionaire bloggers are churning. The niche they are into and the niche you could get into. You’ll learn to find a suitable content for that niche’s audience and target them by learning suitable channels on Day 1 only.

    Day2

    Get writing & earning

    Learn how to write blogs, pillar articles, list articles and content for social media. Go in dept to further know how to write good content for Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, LinkedIn, and get fabulous engagement on it. Find various ways to promote your content and drive traffic to it, thereby increasing your chance to make money.


    Day3

    Plan: Making that awesome website of yours

    This day will be the most interesting day as it will cover a complete trip from buying that domain name, to hosting and eventually creating that awesome blogging page/website of yours. Learn all the interesting tools that will come super handy to you while developing content. And by the time you are done with this day, you will have your first content/blog already out.
